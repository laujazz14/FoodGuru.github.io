App Development Plan

  • The Food Guru team will use the AGILE software development methodology to develop the application. Which allows the team to respond to the changing requirements and market conditions. The following are the step-by-step description of how the team will develop the application. First step is Planning, our team will gather to define the project goals and requirements. Next is Designing where our team works on designing the application. Where it needs to identify the features and functionalities. Next is Implementation, where our team works on developing the application. Next is Testing and integration where our team works to test the application to ensure that it meets the goals of the project. Lastly is the Maintenance our team will maintain the application to ensure that the application continues to function correctly and efficiently and effectively.

    • Information Architecture or Data Structure

    • The user's personal information is essential to this application and is also one of the data that will be collected. It includes, the name, age, gender, the height, weight, and BMI of the user. Moreover, we should also collect information about nutrition education in cardiovascular system. This information will be collected from the website of the Department of Science and Technology. The user's personal information will be collected base from the user's input from the form. All the scores and progress of the users in the game will also be collected and will be the foundation to see if the users have learned from the game.

    Marketing and Scalability

  • The Food Guru will have 3 stages to market our app and generate users. The three stages are Awareness, Acquisition, and Retention. We will create awareness as much as possible by building our ideal user persona. We want to know what they do, where do they work, what knowledge do they have when it comes to nutrition education, and how do they serve meals for their children. Also, part of our awareness stage is competitor research, we will find potential competitors and understand their product and marketing tactics and once we've understood our competitors, we will be able to differentiate our app from our competitors and help us develop our effective marketing plan which will make us unique from all our competitors. Afterwards is the Acquisition Stage this stage the app is already on the store, and we want to increase our app's discoverability and generate more downloads. We are going to apply the Influencer Marketing on this Acquisition stage. The last stage of our marketing is the Retention stage. Our goal on this stage is to hook up our users to our app and make them use it on a frequent basis. The specific engagement that we are going to use on this stage is the Gamification.

    • Sustainability Plan

  • Our team will still sustain the app even after the FLExPHD competition by following our marketing and scalability goals. We'd still implement our acquisition and retention plans so that we'll have more users and installations in our app. We would also focus on developing a good Customer Service to sustain our app and its users. Providing a good customer support is the key to create awesome word of mouth and fewer negative reviews. Our team will be active on reading the comments and suggestions and we'll actively reply to solve the user's problems with the app. Through our active support team, our users will be more inclined to download and use the Food Guru app, we will make it easy for users to connect and share their feedback with our team. We’ll have a feedback button in our app which could connect with our chat support or email.
