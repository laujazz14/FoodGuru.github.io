Welcome to FoodGuru, "Healthier future, where every Filipino can thrive through nourishing food choices."

Cardiovascular disease has been the leading cause of deaths among Filipinos for how many years and this is due to Filipino's lack of nutrition education which leads to poor eating habits and for decades, it has been passed from one generation to another and the unhealthy cycle continues. There were already 48,728 Filipinos from January to June 2022 who died because of heart disease (Philippine Statistics Authority, 2022; Sales, Lin, & Palaniappan, 2020). According to Statista (2023), eating a lot of meat has been associated with an increased risk of heart disease. Most of the Filipinos are not fond of plant-based foods that's why they most likely develop cardiovascular disease due to high intake of carbohydrates, cholesterol, and salt in their body. Mbuya, Nkosinathi, Demombynes, Piza, and Adona (2021) stated that the prevalence of undernutrition due to lack of nutrition education in the Philippines has remained stagnant for almost three decades with hardly any noticeable improvements. The Food Guru wants to introduce a solution to help shift the food choices of Filipinos toward a healthy diet, a sustainable environment, and a just food system for all by targeting Filipino parents and children in Davao City to use our app.

Solution & Innovation

Nutrition education is essential in promoting heart health and reducing the risk of cardiovascular disease. FOOD GURU is a solution to our problem. It is a game app that provides an engaging nutritional education that supports cardiovascular health system. Moreover, with the help of Food Guru, the new generation will learn about the importance of healthy diet, making healthy food choices, making a nutritious meal plan, improves their overall diet quality, reduce their intake of saturated fats and trans fats, and increase their consumption of food and practice regular exercise. This game provides nutrition education about nutritional values of food, sustainability, and the daily nutritional guide pyramid for Filipinos depending to the set BMI. This game displays the importance of healthy foods to our body. The user experience design and flow of the application is a user-friendly, easy to use, accessible and provides a positive experience for the user. To use this app, The user requires to press the play button and input personal information (Name, age, gender) and weight and height to compute the BMI automatically. The user requires to follow the quest of the game to have a healthy BMI. To have more food storage, the user requires to explore the country and learn more nutritional facts and food crops of the country.
